Project

The Morning After

Work

As a young teen easily absorbed by the widespread of teen angst television, I wanted to create a mini photo series that brought my OC/Degrassi fantasies to life. I came up with "The Morning After" in which I asked some friends to re-enact various stages and interactions of how one recoups from the morning after a party. Whether it be facing their walk of shame, chillin' at waffle house, regretting past mistakes, or continue celebrating, I feel that the day after of celebratory vulnerability holds a certain significance.

Project

The Reach Campaign

Work

In 2013, I was approached to develop the personal brand of the Student Government Executive ticket, Reach. I customized and developed a Wordpress site as a form of outreach to the student body that updated the ticket's journey. I oversaw the branding development through the duration of the campaign which included designing and maintaining brand consistency through various social media platforms.

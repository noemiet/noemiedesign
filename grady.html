Grady Centennial Logo

Work

What started out as a t-shirt contest, developed into something greater. The Grady College held a contest looking for a shirt re-design to celebrate the Grady Centennial. The winning design would be placed on t-shirts for newly-admitted Grady students, and the designer will be sent to an all expense paid trip to New York City and attend the Peabody Awards. Needless to say, I was pretty ecstatic to being flying to NYC for the first time. However, due to the great response on the design, nothing brought me greater joy than seeing my design on the wall
